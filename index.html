The Problem: Freight Pricing Is Broken

Spot freight is a live auction environment. Agents must quote fast while balancing win-rate, margin, carrier volatility, and market swings—usually with incomplete information.

The result is systematic margin leakage.

Even a $30–$50 misprice per load compounds across thousands of shipments.

Winner’s Curse

Win the load—then discover the bid was below cost.

Quote Latency

Manual lane research takes 10–15 minutes; the load is gone.

Agent Variance

Two agents quote two prices for the same lane.

How AI DealGuard Works

A human-in-the-loop pricing copilot: structured deal entry, instant margin & risk simulation, and an approve/reject/counter recommendation with a clear rationale.

Step 1
Deal Entry

Deal size, customer type, product/service, requested discount.

Step 2
Margin & Risk Simulation

Expected margin impact, comparison to similar deals, and risk flags (precedent, outlier discounting).

Step 3
Recommendation

Approve / Reject / Counter + recommended price range + plain-English explanation.

What the AI returns
  • Recommended price range (win vs. margin tradeoff)
  • Margin impact vs target and cost constraints
  • Risk flags (precedent risk, aggressive discounting)
  • Plain-English rationale for approvers
Humans remain in control—AI DealGuard advises, explains, and documents the decision.
